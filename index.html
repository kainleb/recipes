One-Pan Lemon-Garlic Shrimp + Crispy Rosemary Potatoes (Measured)

Serves 3–4 · Oven 450°F

Ingredients — Potatoes

  • Baby potatoes: 1½ lb (680 g), halved (quarter if bigger than ~1½")
  • Olive oil (for potatoes): 2½ tbsp (37 mL)
  • Fresh rosemary (chopped): 1½ tbsp OR dried rosemary: 1½ tsp
  • Kosher salt (for potatoes): 1 tsp (reduce if using fine/table salt)
  • Black pepper (for potatoes): ½ tsp
  • Garlic powder (optional): ½ tsp

Ingredients — Shrimp

  • Raw shrimp (peeled/deveined): 1 lb (454 g), thawed and patted very dry
  • Olive oil (for shrimp): 1½ tbsp (22 mL)
  • Garlic: 5 cloves, minced (about 1½ tbsp minced)
  • Lemon zest: 1 tsp (from ~1 lemon)
  • Lemon juice: 1½ tbsp (22 mL), plus more to finish
  • Dried oregano (optional): ½ tsp
  • Red pepper flakes (optional): ¼ tsp
  • Kosher salt (for shrimp): ½ tsp
  • Black pepper (for shrimp): ¼ tsp

Finish (optional but nice)

  • Butter (optional): 2 tbsp, melted (or drizzle olive oil)
  • Dried parsley: 1 tsp
  • Lemon wedges: 1 lemon, cut into wedges

Method (timed so nothing overcooks)

  1. Heat oven to 450°F. Put a sheet pan in the oven while it heats (helps crisp).
  2. Toss potatoes with 2½ tbsp olive oil, rosemary, 1 tsp kosher salt, ½ tsp pepper (and optional ½ tsp garlic powder). Spread on the hot pan cut-side down in a single layer.
  3. Roast 20 minutes, then flip/shake the pan and roast another 8–12 minutes until browned and fork-tender (total ~28–32 minutes depending on size).
  4. While potatoes roast, thaw shrimp (cold water 10–15 minutes if needed), drain, and pat very dry.
  5. Toss shrimp with 1½ tbsp olive oil, garlic, 1 tsp lemon zest, 1½ tbsp lemon juice, ½ tsp kosher salt, ¼ tsp pepper, and optional oregano + red pepper flakes.
  6. When potatoes are done, push them to the sides and add shrimp in the center in one layer.
  7. Roast shrimp 6–8 minutes until just pink/opaque.
  8. Finish: drizzle 2 tbsp melted butter (optional), sprinkle 1 tsp dried parsley, and squeeze extra lemon over everything. Serve with lemon wedges.
