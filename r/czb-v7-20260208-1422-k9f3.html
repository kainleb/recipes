CZB-v7

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Ziti (or Penne)
  • 1.5 lbs Boneless skinless chicken breast, cut into bite-sized medallions
  • 2 large heads of Broccoli, cut into small florets
  • 4-6 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup Dry white wine (Pinot Grigio or Sauvignon Blanc)
  • 1 cup Chicken stock (low sodium)
  • 6 tbsp Unsalted butter, cut into cubes
  • 1/2 cup Freshly grated Pecorino Romano or Parmesan
  • Red pepper flakes, salt, and black pepper to taste
  • 0.5 cup starchy pasta water (reserved)

Instructions

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the ziti. Three minutes before al dente, add the broccoli florets directly to the pot.
  2. Heat a large skillet with olive oil over medium-high heat. Season chicken with salt and pepper, sear until golden and cooked through. Remove and set aside.
  3. Reduce heat to medium, add 1 tablespoon butter, and sauté garlic and red pepper flakes for about 1 minute.
  4. Deglaze with white wine, scraping up fond. Reduce by about half. Add chicken stock and simmer 3-4 minutes.
  5. Turn heat to low and whisk in cold butter, one cube at a time, to create an emulsion.
  6. Drain pasta and broccoli (reserve pasta water). Add to the skillet with the chicken, toss with cheese to emulsify. Add pasta water as needed.

Keys to Success

Use freshly grated cheese and avoid boiling the sauce once butter and cheese are added to prevent breaking.
