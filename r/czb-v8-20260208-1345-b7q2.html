CZB-v8

Prep 15 mins | Cook 15 mins | Serves 4

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Ziti (or Penne)
  • 1.5 lbs Boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 2 large heads of Broccoli
  • 4-6 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup dry white wine
  • 1 cup chicken stock
  • 6 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup grated Pecorino Romano or Parmesan
  • Red pepper flakes, salt, and black pepper
  • 1/2 cup starchy pasta water

Instructions

  1. Cook pasta and broccoli together.
  2. Sear the chicken.
  3. Sauté garlic and pepper flakes.
  4. Deglaze and simmer with stock.
  5. Emulsify with cold butter.
  6. Toss with pasta, cheese, and pasta water.
